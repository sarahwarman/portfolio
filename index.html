About Me

From a young age, I've been driven by a belief that "there's always room for improvement" and that "it doesn't have to be this hard." These guiding principles were shaped early on, when as a child at summer camp, I watched someone struggle needlessly and learned the value of speaking up and seeking simpler solutions. This moment has profoundly influenced my approach to UX design—always advocating for the user and striving to make complex systems more accessible and intuitive.

With over four years of academic study and multiple certifications in UX design, my expertise is underpinned by a strong foundation in psychology and a commitment to diversity. These disciplines not only enhance my design perspective but also enrich my understanding of user interactions and the importance of inclusive design.

Throughout my career, I've consistently leveraged the right tools to transform challenges into learning opportunities, ensuring that every project not only meets but exceeds expectations. My work is a testament to my belief that with the right approach, every system can be improved, making every user's experience more enjoyable and effective.
